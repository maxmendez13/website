Research

Currently, I am conducting research under Dr. Carlos Decena for a study called The Pronouns Project. This study examines the lives of trans and nonbinary students at Rutgers University through interviewing people who are a part of and allies to this community. The result of this study will be to implement an initiative across the Rutgers New Brunswick, Camden, and Newark campuses that will support trans and nonbinary lives.
